Drill Team



JROTC cadets learn drill for the same reasons that soldiers,sailors, airmen, and marines do.
Drill is a time-tested laboratory for developing leadership skills.You stand taller because of drill.
You develop a sense of pride about yourself and that pride translates into success in other areas of your life.
LHS JROTC during drill trains for a variety of things such as following standarized marching,
Drill competitions, following commands given from the Drill Commander, and marching in local parades.
