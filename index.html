LHS AFJROTC


AFJROTC is a 4-year program for high school students. The curriculum includes Aerospace science(AS), Leadership Education(LE) and Wellness. The objective of the class is to motivate cadets to lead healthly, active lifestyles beyond program requirements and into their adult lives.
