The effects of the JROTC program/activties reach far beyond the classroom and into the community
in developing character, leadership, and civic responsibility in tens of thousands of Amercian children.

JROTC at its essence is a character education progam. The program keeps students in school, helps them find their
way during the turbulent teenage years, and assists them in becoming productive memebers of their community.

The LHS JROTC program provides young men and women who are ready to accept the responsibilities of bettering their community.

In only one year the entire core of LHS JROTC completed over 1,000 hours of community service.
